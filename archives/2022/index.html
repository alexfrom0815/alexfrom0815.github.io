About Me

This is Hang Zhao, a 23-year-old third-year Computer Scicence doctoral student of the National University of Defense Technology in China, and my supervisors are Prof. Kai Xu and Prof. Xin Xu. Since June 2021, I have become a visiting member of LAMDA group (led by Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou) under the guidance of Prof. Yang Yu, at Nanjing University. Before these, I received a bachelor’s degree in Electronic Information Engineering from the Beijing Institute of Technology.

I am also looking for high-quality academic cooperation. If you are interested or have any problems, please feel free to contact me at alexfrom0815@gmail.com.
