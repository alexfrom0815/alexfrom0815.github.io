About Me

This is Hang Zhao （赵航）, a 24-year-old fourth-year Computer Scicence doctoral student of the National University of Defense Technology in China, and my supervisors are Prof. Kai Xu and Prof. Xin Xu. Since June 2021, I have become a visiting member of LAMDA group (led by Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou) under the guidance of Prof. Yang Yu, at Nanjing University. Before these, I received a bachelor’s degree in Electronic Information Engineering from the Beijing Institute of Technology.

I am also looking for academic cooperation. If you are interested or have any problems, please feel free to contact me at alex.hang.zhao@gmail.com (WeChat: alex_hang_zhao).
