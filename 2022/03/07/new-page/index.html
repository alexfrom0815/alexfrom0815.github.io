About Me

I am Hang Zhao （赵航）, currently conducting postdoctoral research at Wuhan University. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2025 from NUDT, under the supervision of Professor Kai Xu. Upon the recommendation of Professor Xin Xu, I served as a visiting researcher in LAMDA group led by Professor Zhi-Hua Zhou at Nanjing University, under the guidance of Professor Yang Yu, from June 2021 to March 2024. Prior to my doctoral studies, I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Electronic Information Engineering from the Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT).

I am also looking for academic cooperation. If you are interested or have any problems, please feel free to contact me at alex.hang.zhao@gmail.com (WeChat: alex_hang_zhao).

Research Interests

Industrial Embodied Intelligence, Reinforcement Learning, Combinatorial Optimization
