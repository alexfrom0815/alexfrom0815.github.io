About Me

I am Hang Zhao （赵航）, currently a postdoctoral researcher at Wuhan University, working in collaboration with Professor Kai Xu from NUDT. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2025 from NUDT, under the supervision of Professor Kai Xu. Upon the recommendation of Professor Xin Xu, I served as a visiting researcher in LAMDA group led by Professor Zhi-Hua Zhou at Nanjing University, under the guidance of Professor Yang Yu, from June 2021 to March 2024. Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed my Bachelor’s degree at the Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT).

We are hiring PhD, Msc, and visiting students in embodied intelligence. Our group is research-driven, offering active academic mentoring, ample computational resources, and a focus on valuable, exciting research topics. Students working with us have published in top-tier venues such as SIGGRAPH and RSS, or received perfect review scores for their papers. If you are interested, please drop me an email at hangzhao@whu.edu.cn, alexfrom0815@gmail.com (WeChat: alex_hang_zhao).

Research Interests
